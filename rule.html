    Youth Droneball(Class 20)

    SKYKICK

    Structure A spherical outer structure
    Diameter Diameter of about 20cm±1cm
    Weight Less than 110g
    The balls are distinguished by different  LED lights and so on

    Dronefence

    The size of the stadium should be in the shape of
    a rectangle with one side measuring
    4m and the other side measuring 8m,
    while maintaining a ratio of 2:1 between
    the longer and shorter sides or something very close to that.

    Goal


    A round goal post with diameter of 60cm(inner diameter)

  • Standard Rule

    Standard
    Federation of international dronesoccer association

    Standard Droneball(Class 40)

    D-SOCCER

    Structure A spherical outer structure
    Diameter about 40cm ±2cm
    Weight Less than 1,100g
    The balls are distinguished by different  LED lights and so on

    Dronefence

    TThe size of the rectangular sky fence shall be 5m ~ 10m on the short side and 10m ~ 20m on the long side, and the ratio of the long side to the short side should be 1:2 or in close approximation.

    Goal


    A round goal post with diameter of 60cm(inner diameter)

    Dronesoccer Rules & Regulations

    Each team consists of five players. Two teams maneuver their own drone ball,
    driving it toward the opponent’s goal post while defending their own.
    Only the striker from each team can score goals.
    A team is considered to have forfeited a game if it has fewer than three players including
    the Striker in the pilot area before the game begins despite having
    received ample advance notice of the game starting time.
    In the second or third set of the game, if 3 or more players are not in the pilot area
    before the start of the set, the set is considered lost and
    the start of maintenance time for the next set is declared.

    Score

    - If the Striker's drone ball passes completely from front to back into the opponent's goal, it is admitted as a goal.
    - A goal will not be counted if the player is offside at the time or if the drone ball
    is bounced back after failing to pass completely.

    Defense

    - The defensive players may pass through their own goal post but only from the front, not from the back.
        - It will be considered reverse passing if more than half of  the drone ball entered the goal post before it was pulled  back during the process of defending the goal post.
      - When defending in front of goal, if more than half of the  drone balls enter the goal and then come back out, it is  considered a reverse pass.
    - In the event of reverse passing, the opponent’s team  will be allowed a penalty kick.
